Biography

I am working on "Wireless UltraSound Powered Diamond based Neural Stimulators.(SonoNeuroS)

Project: SonoNeuroS (link), aims at developing wireless and battery free ultrasound-based stimulators based on Piezoelectric Micro-Machined Ultrasonic Transducer (p-MUT) technology. A Neural stimulator will be tested after development to analyze the effect of electrical stimulation on the output of retinal ganglion cell. Project lead by Dr. Sahil Sharma, Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral European Fellow, Ultrasound Instrumentation and Biosensors Researcher, under the supervisor of Dr. Clement Hebert and Dr.Homaira Nawabi's "Central nervous system: from development to regeneration" team. Additionally, during a 6-month secondment at ESYCOM Paris under the supervision of Dr. Lionel Rousseau, he will design a fully diamond and Piezoelectric Micro-Machined Ultrasonic Transducer (p-MUT) technology-based neural implants..

Wireless Neural Implants " Team at Grenoble Institute of Neurosciences, INSERM U1216, France

Background

I am a scientist, innovator, researcher, and prestigious Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellow at Inserm U1216. I am dedicated to the advancement of neural recording instrumentation and biomedical technologies.

I obtained my PhD in physical sciences, with a focus on ultrasound and biomedical instruments. My academic career began with a B.Sc. in Sciences, followed by an M.Sc. in Electronics, all of which established the way for my skills in hardware instrumentation and software development. My postdoctoral research involves pioneering work in biosensing for neural recording systems, where I built sophisticated hardware and software solutions to improve data precision and consistency. I've received recognition for my ability to encourage and guide emerging researchers, establishing a collaborative and innovative research environment. My strong analytical skills and proactive mindset have always resulted in favourable project outcomes and improvements in our field of work. Throughout my career, I've been dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technical science and technology. My research addresses current scientific difficulties and provides the basis for future discoveries. I am fluent in English and adept in several programming languages, therefore I am skilled to continue making significant contributions to the scientific community.

I grew up in a small village in India, which is challenging to navigate on Google Maps. My inspiration has formed my enthusiasm for various and dynamic environments. My life story has demonstrated the ability of technological innovation to drive positive social change, pushing me to democratise and diversify STEM education around the world. Through outreach and mentoring, I hope to inspire the next generation of scientists and engineers to pursue meaningful careers in STEM.
